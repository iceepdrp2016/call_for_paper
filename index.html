Dear Colleague,

You are cordially invited to the International Conference on Earthquake Engineering and Post Disaster Reconstruction Planning (ICEE-PDRP 2016) to be held in Bhaktapur, Nepal, from April 24 to 26, 2016. Please submit your latest research work for presentation within the specified time. Submitted conference papers will be reviewed by technical committees of the conference and, all the ICEE-PDRP 2016 accepted papers will be published in ICEE-PDRP 2016 proceedings.

ICEE-PDRP 2016, http://icee-pdrp2016.com, aims at bringing the international researchers, the academicians and the professionals, who are interested in scientific divulgation in the areas of Earthquake Engineering and Post Disaster Reconstruction Planning to share their experiences and original research findings on those topics.

Important Dates:
Deadline for abstract submission October 15, 2015
Notification of abstract acceptance 10 days after submission
Deadline of paper submission January 31, 2016
Deadline for camera ready paper February 29, 2016
Deadline for early registration February 29, 2016

Paper(s) should be original, have technical quality, not have been published elsewhere and written in English. Plagiarism in any form is not allowed; the authors should be responsible for their papers themselves seriously.

Download Abstract Template: ICEE-PDRP2016_ABSTRACT_FORMAT.doc

Further information is available at URL: http://icee-pdrp2016.com

We kindly request you to mark the ICEE-PDRP 2016 date in your calendar (April 24-26, 2016) and inform other colleagues, who might be interested in attending the conference.

We would appreciate your cooperation and look forward to welcoming you in Bhaktapur, Nepal, in April 2016.
